Our Process

Every detail thoughtfully considered

1

Initial Consultation

We begin with a conversation to understand your vision, style preferences, and the story you want to tell. This can be in person, over video call, or through detailed emails.

2

Planning & Preparation

I'll provide guidance on locations, timing, outfits, and all the details that will make your session perfect. For weddings, we'll create a detailed timeline together.

3

The Session

On the day of your session or wedding, my approach is relaxed and unobtrusive. I'll guide you when needed but mostly capture natural, authentic moments as they unfold.

4

Editing & Delivery

Each image is carefully edited with my signature timeless style. You'll receive your gallery within the promised timeframe, presented in an elegant online gallery.
